Generative Art

Maquilishuat

Each text becomes the seed of a unique tree, with organic branches and the blossoms characteristic of the maquilishuat. The same text always produces the same tree.

The maquilishuat (Tabebuia rosea) is the national tree of El Salvador, renowned for becoming covered in pink and lilac blossoms. This piece translates each text's ID into the tree's form, color, and flowering.
