About

I'm Emerson Torres, a Salvadoran mathematician who graduated with honors from the University of El Salvador. I'm passionate about teaching, research, and applying mathematics to science and technology.

I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics at the Instituto de Matemática, Estatística e Ciência da Computação of the Universidade de São Paulo, under Professor Ernesto G. Birgin.

I've accumulated over 1,300 hours of teaching experience in mathematics and science. I've also attended international schools including CIMPA and EMALCA. Spanish is my native language; I hold an intermediate level of English (TOEFL iBT: 71) and am currently learning Portuguese.

Beyond mathematics, I enjoy video editing, graphic design, music production, and programming (mainly Python and C).

Research Interests
